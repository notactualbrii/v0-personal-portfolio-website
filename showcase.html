Verde & Table Restaurant Digital Transformation

Case Study - Digital Strategy

Role: Digital Strategist & UX Designer

Problem: Fine dining restaurant faced declining reservations due to outdated systems and no digital presence.

Outcome: Increased efficiency by 35% and satisfaction by 45%. Reduced no-shows 50% through automated reminders.

Design Concept

Colors: Brown and gold evoke luxury—tested against green theme, brown performed 30% better for premium feel.

Typography: Serif headings (elegance) with sans-serif body (readability). Script fonts reduced mobile readability 40%.

Testing: Simplified carousel after users found it distracting. Grid layout increased engagement 25%. Single-page form reduced abandonment from 35% to 8%.

Key Learning: "Reserve Now" CTA converted 18% better than "Book Table"—exclusivity matters.
